Your tasks, your calendar, one app — powered by on-device AI that keeps your data private. Lazyflow is free, open source, and built for people who want to get things done without subscriptions or cloud lock-in.

AI-POWERED PRODUCTIVITY
- Quick Capture: jot down rough notes and let AI extract structured tasks with due dates, priorities, and subtasks
- Next Up: a single focused task suggestion that learns what you skip, snooze, and complete
- Morning Briefing: start each day with a personalized plan based on your schedule and priorities
- Daily Summary with automatic carryover from yesterday
- Automatic categorization powered by Apple Intelligence
- Connect Ollama or custom AI providers for advanced workflows
- AI runs on-device by default — your data stays on your phone

FOCUS MODE
- Immersive full-screen timer with animated progress ring
- Pomodoro mode with configurable work and break intervals
- Subtasks and notes panel — see everything without leaving focus
- Lock in on one task at a time with no distractions
- Sessions persist across app restarts — pick up where you left off
- Track focus streaks and build consistent habits

CALENDAR INTEGRATION
- Dedicated Lazyflow calendar with two-way task-event sync
- Day view (default on iPhone) and week view with persistent preference
- Set scheduled start and end times for event-like task planning
- Tap any event to instantly create a task
- Supported recurring tasks sync as recurring calendar events

TASK MANAGEMENT
- Task lists for projects and categories with custom colors and icons
- Due dates, smart reminders, and recurring tasks
- Priority levels with color coding
- Subtasks with automatic completion tracking
- Swipe actions for quick edits
- Full task history with search and filters

SIRI SHORTCUTS
- "Add a task in Lazyflow"
- "Complete my next task"
- "What's on my agenda today"
- Hands-free task management

WIDGETS & LIVE ACTIVITIES
- Home Screen widgets (Small, Medium, Large)
- Lock Screen Live Activity with Dynamic Island support
- Real-time progress updates throughout your day

APPLE WATCH
- Today's tasks on your wrist
- Tap to complete tasks
- Real-time sync with iPhone
- Watch complications for at-a-glance progress

iPAD OPTIMIZED
- Sidebar navigation with adaptive layouts
- Full keyboard shortcuts
- Split View multitasking

PRIVACY FIRST
- All data stored locally on your device
- Optional iCloud sync across your devices
- No tracking, no analytics, no ads
- AI runs on-device by default with Apple Intelligence
