Lazyflow is a free, open-source, calendar-first todo app that makes task scheduling as natural as writing a todo.

KEY FEATURES

Task Management
- Create, edit, and organize tasks with ease
- Task lists for projects and categories
- Due dates with smart reminders
- Recurring tasks for habits and routines
- Priority levels with color coding
- Subtasks with auto-completion

Calendar Integration
- Native Apple Calendar sync
- Day and week calendar views
- See events alongside tasks
- Swipe events to create tasks
- Schedule tasks as time blocks

AI-Powered Productivity
- Powered by Apple Intelligence
- 100% on-device, private, and free
- Smart task suggestions
- Automatic categorization
- "What should I do next?" recommendations

Siri Shortcuts
- "Add a task in Lazyflow"
- "Complete my next task"
- "What's on my agenda today"
- Hands-free task management

Widgets & Live Activities
- Home Screen widgets (Small, Medium, Large)
- Lock Screen Live Activity
- Dynamic Island support
- Real-time progress updates

Apple Watch
- Today's tasks on your wrist
- Tap to complete tasks
- Real-time sync with iPhone
- Watch complications

iPad Optimized
- Sidebar navigation
- Adaptive layouts
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Split view multitasking

Privacy First
- All data stored locally on device
- Optional iCloud sync
- No tracking or analytics
- AI runs entirely on-device with Apple Intelligence
